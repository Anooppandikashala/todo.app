Smart Date Grouping
Tasks auto-sorted into Overdue, Today, Tomorrow, This Week, and Later - always know what demands attention right now.
Priority Levels
Color-coded Low, Medium, High with vivid left-border accent strips on every card. Spot what's urgent without reading a word.
Categories & Labels
Organize with color-coded categories - Personal, Work, Shopping, Health. Filter the list to any category in one tap.
Search & Filter
Instant full-text search with live results combined with category filter chips for fast, precise control over your list.
Task Notes
Add multi-line notes to any task. A one-line preview appears right on the card - extra context, always at a glance.

Overdue tasks float to the top with bold red date badges and a dedicated section. Nothing slips through the cracks.
Undo Actions
Accidentally deleted or completed a task? A Snackbar undo snaps it right back - one tap and it's like it never happened.

Export your entire task list to a file and restore it on any device anytime. Your data stays yours, always.
